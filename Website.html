About Iggy

Iggy is a tech entrepreneur and investor with primary focus on Blockchain and AI technologies. With an extensive background in finance and economics, Iggy's strengths lie in data analysis and research. His main research interests are in blockchain, cryptocurrency and Initial Coin Offerings (ICO) where he has produced some investment reports and written a few articles. Iggy is also a managing partner of Sapphire Capital - a boutique hedge fund that focuses on alternative assets. His investment portfolio consists of cryptocurrencies, unit trusts, real estate and alternative investments.

As a tech fanatic and through his academic studies, Iggy has exposure to basic programming across a variety of languages such as R, python, solidity, java script, html, css, eViews and stata. Using these languages Iggy has conducted economic and financial research, and participated in machine learning and blockchain development projects.

Iggy is a vibrant character with an interest in a variety of activities. When he's not watching the ebbs and flows of his Blockfolio, he can be found at the Armoury trying to drop that punching bag or taking pictures at one of Cape Town's beautiful mountains. Iggy also enjoys spending time with friends, travelling and meeting new people. Previously an oenophile, Iggy now prefers some fine whisky and the deep philosophical conversations that come along with it.

Professional Background

Iggy holds a bachelor's degree in economics from the University of Cape Town (UCT) and a master's in finance from Wits Business School.
