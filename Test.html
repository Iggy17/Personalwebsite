Ideation & Team Formation

Our idea to create a blockchain based title deed solution emerged from an impromptu pitch session. Several other interesting ideas were considered, however, after extensive deliberation we can to a conclusion that a blockchain based title deed solution will have the greatest social impact especially for the underpriviledged communities.
